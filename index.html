Chapter I

Our Story

From classmates
to soulmates

It all began in the corridors of their college — two strangers brought together by the same classroom, the same chaotic canteen queues, and the same love for Kerala's endless monsoon afternoons. What started as borrowed notes and shared laughter slowly grew into something neither of them had planned for.

Through college fests and quiet evenings by the backwaters, through shared chai and unspoken glances, friendship quietly bloomed into love. The kind that sneaks up on you — warm and certain, like the first rain on Kerala soil.

"We started as friends, we fell in love in between, and now we begin forever."
